About TAO: Conveniently located inside The Venetian Resort and Casino, TAO Asian Bistro is an unparalleled restaurant in the heart of the Las Vegas Strip. Stepping into the space transports guests from Las Vegas into a pan-Asian experience complete with a Japanese Koi-filled infinity pool, lush silk draperies, and century-old stones to complete the incredible ambiance. Every table in the 400-seat, two-level dining area is placed under the watchful eyes of our 20-foot Buddha statue, whose presence ensures every guest departs feeling completely satisfied.
